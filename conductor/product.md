# Initial Concept

Automate the intake of **NYC Police Accident Reports** into **Clio Manage** to improve "speed-to-lead" and reduce manual paralegal workload from 45 minutes to under 2 minutes per case for **Andrew Richards** (Richards & Law).

---

# Product Guide

## Vision
The vision for this project is to create a seamless, AI-powered intake automation system for Richards & Law, a personal injury law firm in NYC. By automating the extraction of data from police reports and integrating it with Clio Manage, the firm will significantly reduce lead response times, ensuring a competitive advantage in securing new clients.

## Target Users
- **Andrew Richards (Managing Attorney):** Primary stakeholder who needs high-level oversight and improved conversion rates.
- **Paralegals / Intake Specialists:** Operational users who currently handle manual data entry and need to reduce their workload while maintaining accuracy.

## Core Goals
1. **Reduce Lead Response Time:** Turn a 45-minute manual task into a sub-2-minute automated process.
2. **Increase Operational Efficiency:** Automate data entry, document generation, and calendaring.
3. **Enhance Client Experience:** Ensure potential clients receive immediate, personalized communication following an accident.

## Key Features
- **AI-Powered Data Extraction:** Claude API extracts structured data from NYPD Police Accident Reports (MV-104AN).
- **Clio Manage Integration:** Automatic updating of matters, contacts, and custom fields.
- **Automated Document Generation:** Triggering of Clio's native document automation for retainer agreements.
- **Smart Calendaring:** Automatic calculation and scheduling of the Statute of Limitations (SOL).
- **Dynamic Email Communication:** Personalized emails with seasonal scheduling links (In-office vs. Virtual).
- **Human-in-the-Loop Verification:** A simple interface for paralegals to verify and approve extracted data.

## Business Value
- **Higher Conversion:** Faster response means being the first firm to reach the client.
- **Scalability:** The firm can handle a higher volume of intakes without increasing headcount.
- **Accuracy:** Reduction of human error in data entry and critical legal deadline calculations.
